Archives

The Picture Gallery of the URSM and the COS is an archive of photographs and other visual materials that document the history of the university and its College of Science. The collection includes images of past events, ceremonies, faculty members, staff, and students. Archives like the Picture Gallery help to preserve the history and heritage of the institution, maintain a sense of continuity and connection between past and present members of the university community, and establish the university's reputation as a leading academic institution. The collection serves as a valuable resource for researchers, students, and alumni, and can also be used to promote the university to prospective students, donors, and other stakeholders. Overall, the Picture Gallery plays a vital role in preserving and promoting the history and achievements of the University of Rizal System Morong Campus and its College of Science.
