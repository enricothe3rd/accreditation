BS Psychology

The BS Psychology program is a degree program that provides students with an understanding of human behavior and mental processes. The program covers topics such as social psychology, developmental psychology, abnormal psychology, and cognitive psychology. Graduates of the program can pursue careers in research, counseling, education, human resources, and other related fields.

scientific discipline that studies mental states and processes and behaviour in humans and other animals. The discipline of psychology is broadly divisible into two parts: a large profession of practitioners and a smaller but growing science of mind, brain, and social behaviour. The two have distinctive goals, training, and practices, but some psychologists integrate the two.
