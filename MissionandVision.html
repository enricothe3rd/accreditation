Mission, Vision, Core Values, and Quality Policy of the University

The University has a mission to provide quality education, produce competent graduates, and engage in research and community service. Its vision is to be a premier educational institution that nurtures innovative and ethical leaders, promotes social justice, and fosters sustainable development. The University's core values reflect its commitment to academic excellence, social responsibility, integrity, professionalism, and innovation. Its quality policy is to continuously improve the quality of its operations through the implementation of a quality management system that complies with relevant standards and regulations. These guiding principles reflect the University's commitment to producing knowledgeable, skilled, socially responsible, and ethical graduates.
