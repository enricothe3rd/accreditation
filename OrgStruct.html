Organizational Structure

The University of Rizal System Morong Campus is a multi-campus public university in the Philippines that offers various degree programs. The College of Science is one of its main colleges, dedicated to providing quality education and research in the field of science. The College of Science is organized into several departments, each headed by a chairperson who oversees the academic programs. The College of Science has a hierarchical structure with the Dean at the top, followed by the Associate Deans, department chairs, faculty members, and support staff. The organizational structure ensures that the College operates efficiently and effectively in fulfilling its academic and research goals.

URSM-College of Science

The College of Science at the University of Rizal System Morong Campus offers undergraduate and graduate degree programs, certificate programs, and short courses in Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. Students can engage in research activities under the guidance of experienced faculty members. The College provides a strong foundation in its degree programs, covering topics such as calculus, algebra, statistics, biology, chemistry, and physics. The graduate programs are designed to provide advanced knowledge and skills and prepare students for research and academic careers. The College of Science is committed to promoting scientific inquiry and innovation among its students and faculty.
